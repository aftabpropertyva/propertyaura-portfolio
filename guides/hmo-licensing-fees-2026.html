HMO Licensing Fees Explained: 2026 UK Breakdown

Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs) are a popular investment, but licensing fees can significantly impact your yield. Here is what you need to know for 2026.

Types of Licensing

  • Mandatory Licensing: Applies to properties with 5+ occupants from 2+ households.
  • Additional Licensing: Discretionary schemes set by local councils for smaller HMOs.
  • Selective Licensing: Applies to all rental properties in specific areas, regardless of HMO status.

Impact on Yield

A £1,000 licence fee amortised over 5 years adds £200/year to your expenses. While small, when combined with increased compliance costs, it's vital to use an HMO Yield Calculator to ensure your deal still stacks up.
